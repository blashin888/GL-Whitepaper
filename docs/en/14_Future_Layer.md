# Future

The future is not a plan.

It does not need to be defined.

---

It emerges from relation.

From how people move,
pause,
and reconnect.

---

It cannot be forced.

When pushed, it hardens.

When allowed, it softens.

---

There is no single direction.

Only shifts,
formed across time,
distance,
and context.

---

What lasts becomes quiet.

What fits remains.

---

If it works,
it disappears.

It becomes part of how life moves.
