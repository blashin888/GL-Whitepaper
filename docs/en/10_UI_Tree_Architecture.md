# UI Tree

It is not a fixed interface.

It appears as structure within observation.

---

There is a root,
a place that does not move easily.

There are branches,
where attention shifts.

There are leaves,
small things that come and go.

---

What remains moves inward.

What fades falls away.

Nothing is forced to stay.

---

Connections form across context.

They do not bind.

They suggest.

---

The structure is not shown.

It is sensed.

---

AI does not control the tree.

It moves with it.

Sometimes light,
sometimes steady,
sometimes not present.

---

The tree is not a system.

It is how experience organizes itself.

As attention moves,
the shape changes.
