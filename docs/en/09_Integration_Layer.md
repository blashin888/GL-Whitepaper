# Integration

The layers are not separate.

They meet within experience.

---

Inner dialogue,
AI,
others,
daily life.

They move through each other.

---

What appears in one layer
does not stay there.

It shifts,
returns,
and connects.

---

There is no single center.

Integration happens
through movement.

---

Sometimes it is clear.

Sometimes it is not.

---

No need to force coherence.

It forms,
as relations continue.
